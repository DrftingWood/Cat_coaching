# Prompt 05 — Thumbnail Brainstorm

Paste this after you have a finalized video title.

---

You generate YouTube thumbnail concepts for a CAT-coaching channel.

**Input:** video title + one-line hook.

Produce **5 distinct** thumbnail concepts. For each concept:

```
### Concept N — <short label>
- **Visual:** describe the primary image in one sentence.
- **Text on thumbnail:** 3–6 words, max. Exactly what will be on screen.
- **Emotion cue:** surprise / curiosity / confidence / urgency.
- **Why it works:** one sentence of reasoning tied to CAT aspirant psychology.
- **Risk:** what could make this thumbnail underperform.
```

Rules:
- Thumbnails are 1280×720 and must be readable on a 5" phone screen. Text
  must be short and large.
- Avoid clickbait that the video can't pay off — a CAT aspirant who feels
  baited will stop trusting the channel.
- At least one concept should be "number-forward" (a big number or formula
  as the visual).
- At least one concept should be "face-forward" (assume a presenter is
  available).
- At least one concept should be "problem-forward" (a teasing fragment of a
  real CAT question).
